    About:

    Vagator is derived from two different words, 'Vaga' which means tiger and 'tor' which means shore. Due to the close proximity between thickly forested Vagator hills and Sahyadri ranges, many tigers used to visit here in search of sweet water and that is how this place in Goa was eventually named as Vagator.
